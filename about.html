Our Story

Aubin's Bonsai was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves a living work of art in their home. What started as a passion project in a small Takoma Park backyard has grown into a dedicated studio where every tree is shaped by hand with patience and intention.

We specialize in modern mini-bonsai that are approachable for beginners yet beautiful enough to captivate seasoned collectors. Each tree in our collection is nurtured from young stock, carefully pruned and wired over months — sometimes years — before it's ready to find its new home.

🌲

The Artist

Our in-house bonsai artist brings over fifteen years of experience to every tree. Trained in both Japanese and Chinese bonsai traditions, they blend classical techniques with a contemporary eye — creating trees that feel timeless yet fit seamlessly into modern interiors.

Every cut, every bend of wire, every choice of pot is deliberate. The result is a living sculpture that tells a story of patience, care, and the beauty of nature in miniature.

🌿

Quality First

Every tree is individually inspected and shaped before shipping. We never mass-produce — each bonsai is a one-of-a-kind creation.

Beginner Welcome

Every order includes a printed care guide tailored to your specific tree species. We're also always available to answer questions via email.

Ships Safely

We've perfected our packaging to ensure your tree arrives healthy and intact. Every order is shipped with care using custom-designed boxes.
